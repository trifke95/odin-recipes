Easy Coleslaw Dressing

A creamy coleslaw dressing that can be made with ingredients you already have! You can pour it immediately over a 14-ounce package of coleslaw mix or refrigerate until needed.

Ingridients


  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

DIrekctions


  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  3. Step 3: Store in an airtight container.
